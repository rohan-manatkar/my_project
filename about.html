Welcome to typing World.

Typing speed tester is online tool that allow you to measure how fast you can type by assessing your typing speed in words per minute (WPM) or characters per minute (CPM). Here are typically 5 tests that involves typing. Practicing with typing speed tester can help you to improve your typing skills and increase your overall efficiency when using a keyboard.

1st Test :

This is "Typing speed Test" this involves a sentence and you have to type this sentence as quickly and accurately as you can. The result will show your typing speed of words per minutes also total number of words you type and time taken to type those words. Benefits of regularly practicing "Typing Speed Test" is speed improvement helps you track your speed progress over time also focus on improving your typing accuracy.Improving typing speed can significantly increase productivity especially when it comes to tasks that involves lot of typing such as emails or reports.

Game 1 : Word Scramble

This test typically involves a game called "Word Scramble" Here you have to figure out the word from given hint in given time limit. And the result will show if your given answer is correct or not. Suppose the given letters are "R O G P U" , and the given hint is "a number of persons or object" the correct answer is "GROUP" you have to figure out it quick in given time limit. This test help to improve your typing speed and accuracy as you quickly type the unscramble words.This can be particularly beneficial for enchancing your typing skills. Also help in expand your vocabulary and improve your word recognition skills.

2nd Test:

These test typically involve typing a given passage or random words accurately without any mistak. The results will shows your typing of words per minutes out of given words and total number errors you made during the test. This can develop muscle memory in your fingers allowing you to type more efficiently without having to look at keyboard.Many professions required strong typing skills and have fast and accurate typing speed can make you more competitive in the job market.

Game 2 : Word per minute

This is a game this measure how fast you can type by assessing your typing speed in words per minute (WPM). This involve random word that you have to type quickly and accurately as you can within 30-second time frame this shows your score , word per minute (wpm) and time left. By practicing this game help you become a faster and more efficient typist which can beneficial in both personal and professional context.

3rd test :

This test involves a paragraph you have to type each word and punctuation marks accurately without any mistake within 1-minute time frame. This shows your total number of mistakes, words per minute(wpm) and character per minute(cpm). Taking this speed test can be great way to asses and improve typing skills.By praticing regularly with timed tests, you can work on minimizing errors and typos in your typing.

Game : Word Guessing Game

This is "Word Game" you have to type a correct word of a given hint without any mistake you have total 5 chance if you type 5 incorrect letter you will lost. Suppose the given hint is "Move; Period of word" the correct answer is "Shift" you have to type that word accurately if your spell incorrect you will lost the game. This can help expand your vocabulary also can enchance your cognitive abilities such as problem-solving and critical thinking

4th test :

This test involve the paragraph and you have to write paragraph with in 30 second. For starting the game you need to first click on "Click here to focus" button. After that the game is start and you have to complete writing in 30 second.Wrong word are highlighted in red colour and right words are highlighted in green colour.
